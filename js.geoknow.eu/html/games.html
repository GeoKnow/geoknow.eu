Pea Invasion

Multitasking Game

In general, pea invasion is a single player web game implemented in GWT with the underlying goal to validate links in the Web of Data. User your peas to hinder the enemy peas from reaching the left side of the game screen. With every defeated enemy pea, your score will increase. Answer the quiz to earn coins so you can buy new troops. The more coins you have, the better you can defend and achieve a higher score. While answering you compete against the community of all users, which played the game, including those who are currently playing it.

Space Ships

Turn Based Game (Prototype)

As a second showcase game we have implemented a one versus one space ship battle game, written in Javascript. Target of the game is to destroy an enemyâ€™s ship before he destroys yours. The game progresses in turns with each turn taking 30 seconds to complete. During the turn user must verify as many links as possible. Damage done to enemy ship is calculated at the end of the turn and it's based on the number of verified links as well as the precision of verification. It's still under development.
